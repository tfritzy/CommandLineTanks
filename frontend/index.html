COMMAND_SYNTAX

explore
drive

Navigate using pathfinding by specifying a target code, relative coordinate, or cardinal direction.

drive <target|dir|coords>
$ drive northeast 10
ads_click
fire

Discharge current weapon system at turret's current bearing. Respects weapon reload timers.

fire
$ fire
navigation
aim

Rotate your turret to a specific degree (0-360) or named direction.

aim <degrees|direction>
$ aim 90
track_changes
target

Lock onto an enemy unit's ID code. Your turret will automatically track their movement.

target <id_code>
$ target a4
cloud
smoke

Deploy a smoke cloud at your position. Breaks lock-ons and obscures vision.

smoke
$ smoke
bolt
overdrive

Temporarily overclock your engines for a 25% speed increase (10s duration).

overdrive
$ overdrive
swap_horiz
switch

Switch to a specific weapon system slot (1, 2, or 3).

switch <slot>
$ switch 2
build
repair

Initiate armor repair sequence. Tank must remain stationary during repair.

repair
$ repair
list_alt
tanks

List all active units in the current sector with distance and team info.

tanks
$ tanks
